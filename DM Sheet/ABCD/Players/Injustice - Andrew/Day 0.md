## Session Events

### At School

- Injustice stepped in and tried to **gently stop Marcus** from bullying Dillon
- Marcus shifted attention to Injustice, noted it was his birthday, and asked what his favorite cake was
- Injustice said **Carrot Cake**
- Marcus accepted the answer and left — Dillon was not taken

### The Accident

- Grandma was hit in a **reckless driving incident** caused by Marcus
- She is currently in the hospital in **critical care**
- **Doctor Jones** is the attending physician handling her care

### The Investigation

- Police **initially opened an investigation** into the hit and run
- Work on the case gradually stopped with no explanation
- Police have been giving **excuses** when pressed; no progress, no accountability

### The Visit

- A **lawyer and bodyguard representing Breakers Inc** (Marcus's father's company) came to see Injustice
- They offered **$50,000** in exchange for his silence
- When he refused, they raised the offer to **$75,000**
- Injustice refused both offers
- They left a **business card** before leaving
- They warned him **not to speak to anyone** about the incident
