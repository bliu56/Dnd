## Minor Villages and Optional Quests

As the party travels toward Aurelion, they pass through several **border and road villages**. These quests are optional, low commitment, and primarily serve worldbuilding, reputation, and foreshadowing.

## Village 1: Briarhollow

_(BRY-er-hol-oh)_
A small farming village surrounded by thorny hedges and old stone markers.

## Minor Quests

- **Missing Livestock**

    Wolves have been taking sheep, but investigation reveals the wolves are being driven from deeper forest territory by something unnatural.

- **Old War Shrine**

    A neglected shrine to fallen soldiers has begun to glow faintly at night. Restoring it grants minor favor with Aurelion locals.

## Themes

- Lingering scars of the Great War
- Respect for sacrifice

## Village 2: Stoneford Crossing

_(STONE-ford KRAW-sing)_
A trade hamlet built around a heavily guarded bridge.

## Minor Quests

- **Toll Dispute**

    A merchant and local guards argue over forged travel papers tied to a fallen noble house.

- **River Omen**

    Fishermen report dark shapes beneath the water, remnants of demon-tainted creatures flushed out post-war.

## Themes

- Post-war bureaucracy
- Noble corruption echoes

## Village 3: Larkspur Vale

_(LARK-spur VAYL)_
A peaceful village known for music and flower fields.

## Minor Quests

- **The Silent Bard**

    A once-famous bard has lost his voice after encountering something near the old battlefields.

- **Unwanted Suitor**

    A local dispute mirrors old noble marriage politics in miniature.

## Themes

- Healing and unresolved grief
- Social pressure and legacy
