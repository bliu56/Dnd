## 1. Local Store Owner

## Maribel Fenwick

_(MAIR-uh-bel FEN-wik)_
Owner of a small general goods shop near the inner wall.

## Description

Middle-aged woman with tired eyes and a warm smile. Keeps her shop meticulously clean. Lost her husband during the war.

## A: Mistaken for the Hero

Maribel freezes mid-motion when she looks at Edward.
She lowers her voice.

> “I… I thought you’d never come back through these streets.”
She refuses payment for basic supplies.

> “You already paid enough.”
If pressed, she explains her husband died holding a chokepoint long enough for reinforcements to arrive.

> “He believed in you. That was enough for him.”

## B: Normal Interaction

She greets the party cheerfully.

> “Travelers? You picked a good time. Markets are stocked again.”
She offers fair prices and helpful advice.

> “If you’re new, stick to the main avenues at night. Not dangerous, just easy to get lost.”

## 2. Traveling Merchant

## Odrin Halecrest

_(OH-drin HAYL-krest)_
Sells spices, cloth, and minor magical trinkets.

## Description

Well-dressed, fast-talking, with laugh lines and sharp eyes.

## A: Mistaken for the Hero

Odrin squints at Edward, then laughs in disbelief.

> “No. No way. If this is a joke, it’s a cruel one.”
He bows deeply.

> “You made trade possible again. Every road I walk is because of you.”
He offers a steep discount.

> “Heroes don’t haggle. But I insist.”

## B: Normal Interaction

Friendly and businesslike.

> “Aurelion’s open again. That means competition.”
He gives rumors freely.

> “Old demon routes are being cleared. Dangerous work, but profitable.”

## 3. Active Duty Soldier

## Sergeant Bren Talrow

_(BREN TAL-roh)_

## Description

Broad-shouldered, scarred, disciplined. Wears the current Aurelion uniform.

## A: Mistaken for the Hero

Bren snaps to attention immediately.

> “Sir. It’s an honor.”
He speaks carefully, like someone afraid of saying the wrong thing.

> “We train harder because of you. So no one else has to carry that burden alone.”

## B: Normal Interaction

Relaxed but professional.

> “Welcome to Aurelion. Keep weapons peace-bound inside the inner ring.”
He gives directions and warnings.

> “Crowds get thick near the market square.”

## 4. Former Soldier

## Edrik Mossvein

_(ED-rik MOS-vayn)_

## Description

Limping older man with a weathered cloak. Spends his days carving small wooden animals.

## A: Mistaken for the Hero

Edrik stares for a long moment, then sits down heavily.

> “You were real. I knew it.”
He doesn’t bow.

> “I followed you once. From a distance. You didn’t know me.”
He thanks Edward quietly, then says no more.

## B: Normal Interaction

He offers a carved trinket to a child nearby.

> “War’s over. Time to make better things.”
He warns the party.

> “Peace is fragile. Treat it kindly.”

## 5. Elderly Civilian

## Grandmother Ilva

_(ILL-vah)_

## Description

Small, sharp-eyed, wrapped in layers of shawls. Knows everyone’s business.

## A: Mistaken for the Hero

She grabs Edward’s hands firmly.

> “You look thinner.”
She scolds him gently.

> “Retirement means rest. Even heroes must eat properly.”
She sends the party away with food whether they want it or not.

## B: Normal Interaction

She smiles warmly.

> “You look like good children.”
She gives unsolicited advice.

> “Stay together. That’s how we survived.”

## 6. Children

## Twin Siblings: Lysa and Toren

_(LEE-sah / TOR-en)_

## Description

Around ten years old. Playing hero games with wooden swords.

## A: Mistaken for the Hero

They stare in awe.

> “Is it really you?”
They ask rapid questions.

> “Did you really fight the Demon King?”
>
> “Did it hurt?”
They promise to be brave someday.

## B: Normal Interaction

They challenge the party to a mock duel.

> “You’re the bad guys. We’re the heroes.”
They laugh when they lose.

## 7. Family Group

## The Harrowells

Parents and two young children, shopping together.

## A: Mistaken for the Hero

The father bows awkwardly.

> “Because of you, my children grew up with both parents.”
The mother adds softly.

> “Thank you for giving us time.”

## B: Normal Interaction

They smile politely.

> “Welcome to Aurelion.”
The children wave.
