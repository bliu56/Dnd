_(EL-oh-wen)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Early 20s during the Great War
- **Appearance:** Slender and graceful, with long chestnut hair and green eyes. Wears elegant armor when in battle and ceremonial gowns otherwise.
- **Personality:** Compassionate, wise beyond her years, and deeply empathetic. Known for diplomacy and the ability to read people’s emotions. Calm under pressure, but capable of decisiveness when necessary.
- **Relationship to Hero:** Close ally during the war. Fought alongside the hero in key battles and shared a mutual respect. Elowen often acted as a mediator between the hero and the royal faction.

## Training and Skills

- **Diplomacy & Strategy:** Skilled in negotiation and conflict resolution. Can rally troops or mediate disputes.
- **Defensive Combat:** Trained in swordsmanship and light magical wards under the Steadfast Guardian Pillar.
- **Healing & Support Magic:** Learned basic divine and restorative spells from the Sanctum of Solythea’s clergy.
- **Archery (Optional):** Some ranged training under Lady Thessa Windmere.
