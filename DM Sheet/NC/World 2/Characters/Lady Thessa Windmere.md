_(THESS-ah WIND-meer)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Mid-30s during the Great War
- **Appearance:** Slender and graceful, with auburn hair often tied in a practical braid and sharp amber eyes that seem to miss nothing. Wears lightweight armor for mobility, often adorned with intricate embroidery and personal trinkets. Carries a longbow with subtle magical enhancements and a quiver full of expertly crafted arrows.
- **Personality:**

    - **Playful & Mischievous:** Loves gossip, teasing, and uncovering secrets—especially romantic ones. Has a knack for reading people and subtly influencing them.

    - **Motivator & Strategist:** Uses what she knows about people—their loves, fears, or ambitions—to push them to excel. Will arrange “coincidental” encounters or use playful psychological tactics to encourage growth.

    - **Empathetic:** Deeply cares about the people she trains and will never cross into cruelty; she knows the line and edges it tactfully.

    - **Frustrated Romantic Matchmaker:** Often exasperated when she sees two people clearly care for each other but refuse to act. Uses teasing and hints to nudge them together.

- **Reputation:** Respected for her skill and insight, both on and off the battlefield. Known as a charming trickster and clever tactician.

## Skills & Abilities

- **Archery Mastery:** Unmatched precision and range; capable of taking out high-value targets or leading volleys in battle.
- **Stealth & Reconnaissance:** Skilled in tracking, moving unseen, and scouting enemy positions.
- **Psychological Insight:** Expert at reading people and predicting behavior; uses this knowledge in training, leadership, and battlefield tactics.
- **Combat Agility:** Quick, light-footed, and adaptable in skirmishes; can fight with daggers or dual weapons in close quarters if needed.

## Relationship to Others

- **Hero:** Close ally and frequent companion during reconnaissance and tactical missions. Understands the hero’s strengths and weaknesses and often teases or challenges them to grow.
- **Prince Caelum & Princess Mireya:** Teacher and playful mentor. Often encourages them using personal insight, sometimes arranging encounters to motivate or test them.
- **Royal Family & Pillars:** Considered clever and indispensable; her methods sometimes frustrate the more stoic Pillars (like Sir Aldric), but results speak for themselves.
