_(GAR-on STONE-ward)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Early 50s during the Great War
- **Appearance:** Towering and muscular, with short-cropped black hair streaked with gray and steel-gray eyes. Wears heavy armor polished to a mirror shine, often carrying a massive shield engraved with Aurelion’s crest and a longsword at his side.
- **Personality:**

    - **Loyal & Dependable:** Unshakable in his dedication to the king, royal family, and kingdom. Never wavers under pressure.

    - **Calm & Stoic:** Rarely speaks unnecessarily, but when he does, his words carry authority and wisdom.

    - **Patient Teacher:** Encourages perseverance and resilience; believes in leading by example.

    - **Protective:** Views training and leadership as responsibility to safeguard lives, not just win battles.

## Skills & Abilities

- **Defensive Mastery:** Expert in shield use, defensive formations, and holding lines against overwhelming odds.
- **Endurance & Resilience:** Capable of withstanding extreme physical punishment and prolonged combat.
- **Tactical Awareness:** Skilled in battlefield positioning, troop coordination, and countering enemy strategies.
- **Mentorship:** Trains royal family members, soldiers, and recruits in defensive tactics, resilience, and battlefield responsibility.

## Relationship to Others

- **Hero:** Trusted ally in combat; taught defensive coordination and battlefield awareness. Respects hero’s skill and judgment.
- **Royal Family:** Personal protector of the king and children; mentor in defensive combat for Prince Caelum and Princess Mireya.
- **Other Pillars:** Reliable and respected; often the anchor that balances the more vocal or eccentric Pillars.
