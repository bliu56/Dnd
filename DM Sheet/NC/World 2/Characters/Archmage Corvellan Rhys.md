_(kor-VELL-an REES)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Mid-50s during the Great War
- **Appearance:** Lean and slightly frail-looking, with long black hair streaked with silver. Striking violet eyes that seem to pierce into the arcane essence of things. Wears deep blue robes embroidered with magical sigils, often with small enchanted trinkets hanging from his belt or sleeves.
- **Personality:**

    - **Knowledge-Obsessed:** Values magic, theory, and arcane experimentation above all else. Most social conventions, politics, or frivolities hold little interest for him.

    - **Pragmatic Mentor:** Believes in self-motivation; only trains those truly dedicated. Has a “you can bring a horse to water, but you can’t force it to drink” mentality.

    - **Curious Collector:** Loves magical trinkets, artifacts, and enchanted items, often examining them obsessively.

    - **Kind but Detached:** He is pleasant and polite, rarely harsh, but emotionally distant—his focus is always on magical knowledge and personal growth of those willing to commit.


### Skills & Abilities

- **Master of Arcane Magic:** Expert in offensive, defensive, and utility spells. Capable of battlefield-scale magical support and strategic spellcasting.

- **Magical Analysis:** Can quickly identify magical properties, weaknesses, and potential uses of artifacts or enchantments.

- **Tactical Spellcaster:** Understands magical combat strategy and can coordinate magic with troops for maximum efficiency.

- **Mentorship Philosophy:** Trains only those who demonstrate passion and determination; tests dedication before imparting advanced knowledge.


### Relationship to Others

- **Hero:** Recognized the hero’s latent magical abilities. Guided them selectively and monitored their growth, always testing motivation and initiative.

- **Prince Caelum & Royal Family:** Advisor on magical defense and strategy. Less personally engaged, but respected for expertise.

- **Other Pillars:** Friendly but distant. Often debates magical theory with the Last Shadow or Aldric, but uninterested in minor quarrels or gossip.
