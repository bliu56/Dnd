_(KAY-lum)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Mid-20s during the Great War
- **Appearance:** Tall and athletic, with sharp features and calm blue eyes. Wears a mix of practical armor and royal insignia. Brown hair kept short for battle.
- **Personality:** Level-headed, adaptable, and disciplined. Values honor, loyalty, and the welfare of his people. Calm under pressure but capable of decisive action. Possesses a quiet charisma that inspires soldiers and allies alike.
- **Relationship to Hero:** Trusted comrade-in-arms and close friend. Fought alongside the hero in many battles, developing mutual respect and deep camaraderie. Often served as a voice of reason when conflicts arose within the party or royal forces.

## Training and Skills

Prince Caelum is an **all-arounder**, having trained under each of the Five Pillars of Aurelion. His versatility makes him capable in combat, strategy, and magical support.

|Training Source|Skills & Abilities|Combat Style / Specialty|
|---|---|---|
|**Sir Aldric Veynholm (Swordmaster)**|Swordsmanship, dueling techniques, battlefield leadership|Balanced melee combat; able to lead small units effectively; skilled in single combat duels|
|**Lady Thessa Windmere (Master Archer)**|Archery, tracking, ranged combat|Quick reflexes, sharpshooting, capable of combined melee/ranged tactics; skilled in stealth reconnaissance|
|**Archmage Corvellan Rhys (Grand Magician)**|Basic spellcasting, magical theory, warding and support magic|Can cast protective wards, minor offensive spells, and utility magic; skilled at reading magical threats|
|**Sir Garron Stoneward (Steadfast Guardian)**|Defensive combat, shield use, endurance|Highly resilient; able to hold a defensive line; proficient in defensive tactics and troop coordination|
|**The Last Shadow (Secret Pillar)**|Espionage, stealth, infiltration, intelligence gathering|Expert at covert operations, ambush tactics, and information gathering; can move unseen and anticipate threats|

## Abilities and Special Traits

- **Adaptive Combatant:** Can switch seamlessly between melee, ranged, and magical combat depending on battlefield needs.
- **Tactical Awareness:** High-level understanding of battlefield tactics, troop movements, and enemy psychology.
- **Diplomatic Edge:** Polished in negotiation and diplomacy, capable of mediating disputes between nobles or allied kingdoms.
- **Moral Compass:** Deeply values loyalty and honor; often acts as the ethical guide for his peers.

## Relationship to the Hero

- **Trusted Ally:** Fought alongside the hero on the front lines; witnessed the hero’s skills, leadership, and sacrifices firsthand.
- **Close Friendship:** Beyond combat, shares a personal bond; they confide in one another and strategize together.
- **Respect and Faith:** Caelum respects the hero’s judgment and abilities, but is unaware of the hero’s hidden demonic powers.
- **Catalyst for Unity:** Caelum often acted as a bridge between the hero and the royal/noble factions, helping maintain trust and cooperation during the war.
