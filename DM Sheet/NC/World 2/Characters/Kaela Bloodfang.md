_(KAY-lah BLOOD-fang)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Late teens during the Great War
- **Appearance:** Athletic and energetic, with dark brown skin, long black hair often tied in a high braid, and amber eyes that sparkle with curiosity and mischief. Wears light armor suited for mobility and hunting.
- **Personality:**

    - **Outgoing & Charismatic:** Naturally sociable, makes friends easily across all tribes. Loves storytelling, laughter, and inspiring others.

    - **Adventurous:** Always seeking new challenges and experiences, rarely content with staying in one place.

    - **Caring & Loyal:** Deeply protective of her people and her father; will risk herself for the tribe.

    - **Curious & Bold:** Often asks questions others would avoid and isn’t afraid to challenge traditions respectfully.

## Skills & Abilities

- **Combat Skills:** Trained in light melee weapons (spear, short sword) and basic archery. Focuses on agility, speed, and quick reflexes rather than brute strength.
- **Tracking & Hunting:** Skilled in wilderness survival, scouting, and tracking prey or enemies.
- **Diplomacy & Morale:** Her outgoing nature makes her an excellent bridge between tribes; boosts morale and inspires unity.
- **Stealth & Reconnaissance:** Learned minor stealth tactics from her father’s allies, capable of moving unseen when necessary.

## Relationship to Others

- **Varok Bloodfang (Father):** Deeply respects her father but often teases him for being too serious or gruff. Shares a close bond and trains with him in combat.
- **Other Tribal Chieftains:** Known and liked by all chieftains due to her charisma; often mediates light conflicts between tribes.
- **Hero & Allies:** Open and welcoming, she bonds quickly with trusted outsiders; eager to assist or learn from them.
- **Tribal Members:** Viewed as a role model for younger warriors, inspiring both courage and loyalty.
