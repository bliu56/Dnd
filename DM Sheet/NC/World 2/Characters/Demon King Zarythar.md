_(ZAH-ree-thar)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Ancient (exact age unknown, predates all three kingdoms)
- **Appearance:** Towering and imposing, standing over 12 feet tall. Dark crimson skin with black, chitin-like ridges along his shoulders and forearms. Horns curl back from his forehead, eyes burn with a fiery orange glow, and a long, spiked tail flicks menacingly behind him. Wears a jagged obsidian armor fused to his body.
- **Personality:**

    - **Cunning & Ruthless:** Highly intelligent and manipulative; enjoys orchestrating chaos and testing his enemies.

    - **Prideful & Arrogant:** Believes in his superiority over mortals and lesser demons.

    - **Sadistic Strategist:** Takes pleasure in psychological warfare, corrupting kingdoms and sowing distrust among allies.

    - **Calculating:** Rarely acts without a plan; constantly analyzes opponents and potential weaknesses.

## Abilities & Powers

- **Immense Physical Strength:** Able to crush soldiers, break fortifications, and duel the strongest warriors with ease.
- **Demonic Magic:** Masters fire, shadow, and summoning magic. Can call legions of demons to battle, conjure infernal landscapes, and manipulate the environment.
- **Corruption & Possession:** Capable of corrupting mortals, bending them to his will, and inciting betrayal in kingdoms.
- **Regeneration & Durability:** Heals rapidly from injuries that would kill most beings; highly resistant to conventional weapons.
- **Fear Aura:** Instills terror in enemies, lowering morale and weakening their resolve in combat.
