_(AL-drik VAYN-holm)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Late 40s during the Great War
- **Appearance:** Tall and broad-shouldered with a rugged, weathered face. Silver-streaked brown hair tied back, piercing gray eyes, and a perpetual tan from years of training outdoors. Wears masterfully forged plate armor etched with the sigil of Aurelion, though often leaves the helmet off when instructing.
- **Personality:**

    - **Vocal & Carefree:** Loves telling stories, cracking jokes, and lifting the spirits of soldiers. He often teases nobles and recruits alike.

    - **Strict When Training:** Once training begins, he demands perfection, discipline, and focus. He has little tolerance for laziness or half-hearted effort.

    - **Philosophical:** Believes that mastering the sword is as much about understanding oneself as the weapon.

- **Reputation:** Known as both a mentor and entertainer; soldiers respect his skill and humor in equal measure.

## Skills & Abilities

- **Swordsmanship:** Master of a wide variety of weapons, particularly longswords and dual-wielding techniques. Capable of single combat against multiple foes.
- **Tactical Combat:** Skilled in small-unit command and dueling strategy; teaches situational awareness on the battlefield.
- **Mentorship:** Able to identify strengths and weaknesses of students quickly; pushes trainees to their limits.
- **Physical Prowess:** Exceptional strength, endurance, and reflexes; maintains top physical condition despite age.
