_(seh-LAR-een VAL-dor-ee-uh)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Appears early 40s, though her true age is unknown due to divine blessings.
- **Appearance:** Tall and elegant, with long silver hair that seems to shimmer in light. Eyes are a deep violet, appearing almost to see into the soul of those she meets. Wears flowing ceremonial robes adorned with sacred symbols, with a staff topped by a crystal that pulses with faint light.
- **Personality:**

    - **Mysterious & All-Knowing:** Often speaks in riddles or subtle hints, giving the impression that she sees all events before they unfold.

    - **Kind & Compassionate:** Gentle and nurturing toward those who are virtuous, loyal, or seeking guidance sincerely.

    - **Cruel to the Undeserving:** Merciless toward the deceitful, corrupt, or those who abuse power; her judgment is swift and precise.

    - **Calm & Authoritative:** Rarely loses composure; her presence commands respect and reverence.

## Skills & Abilities

- **Divine Magic:** Mastery of healing, protective wards, and holy offensive spells. Can channel the power of the Supreme God.
- **Judgment & Insight:** Able to discern truth, intentions, and hidden corruption through mystical perception.
- **Trial Conductor:** Oversees the **Goddess’ Trial**, a spiritual test to determine the intentions, purity, and moral fortitude of those who seek guidance.
- **Leadership:** Unites all of Solythea’s clergy and army under her command, maintaining order and rooting out corruption.

## Relationship to Others

- **Hero:** Provided guidance and support during the war; respected the hero’s intentions and abilities, though wary of the latent demonic powers.
