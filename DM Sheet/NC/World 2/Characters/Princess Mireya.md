_(mee-RAY-ah)_

## General Description

- **Age:** Late teens during the Great War
- **Appearance:** Athletic build, shorter dark brown hair, amber eyes. Wears practical armor and carries weapons suitable for close combat.
- **Personality:** Fiery, ambitious, and fiercely independent. Often takes initiative in battle and strategic planning. Can be stubborn but highly loyal to family and kingdom.
- **Relationship to Hero:** Admired the hero greatly and often sought to prove herself in combat alongside them. Shared moments of mentorship and rivalry with Prince Caelum.

## Training and Skills

- **Melee Combat:** Expert with twin short swords, trained under Sir Aldric Veynholm.
- **Ranged Combat:** Basic proficiency with bow and javelin.
- **Tactical Thinking:** Trained by Sir Garron Stoneward to coordinate small units in battlefield skirmishes.
- **Stealth & Espionage:** Learned minor techniques from the Last Shadow Pillar, especially reconnaissance.
